## Today's Lab Highlights

Today was all about bridging the gap between theoretical lectures and hands-on practice. I focused on two main areas:

* **Advanced C++ Logic:** Moving beyond "Hello World" to implement conditional structures and loops. It's fascinating to see how code efficiency directly impacts performance.
* **Combinational Circuits:** In **Digital Logic Design**, I worked on more complex gates. Understanding how half-adders and full-adders function is like learning the secret language of the CPU.

## The Engineering Mindset

One thing I realized today is that being a **Computer Engineering** student isn't just about knowing facts; it's about a specific way of thinking. 

1.  **Systematic Debugging:** Instead of guessing, I'm learning to trace logic step-by-step.
2.  **Hardware Constraints:** In software, we often think resources are infinite, but in hardware, every gate and every bit of memory counts.
