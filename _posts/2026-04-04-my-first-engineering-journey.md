# Why Computer Engineering?

My name is **Jamshaid Ali** (Roll No: 2025-BSCPE-165), and I recently embarked on a journey into the world of **Computer Engineering**. People often ask why I chose this field. For me, it was the fascination with how a simple piece of silicon can execute billions of instructions per second to power the world.

## My First Technical Milestone

In my first semester, I began diving into **Programming Fundamentals** and **Digital Logic Design**. One of my favorite experiences so far has been:

* **Learning C++:** Understanding how memory management works at a low level.
* **Circuit Simulation:** Building my first basic logic gates and seeing them function in a simulator.

## Challenges Faced

Engineering isn't always easy. Debugging code for hours or trying to find a loose wire in a physical circuit can be frustrating. However, the "Aha!" moment when the project finally works makes every second of the struggle worth it.

> "The analytical skills I am developing today are the tools I will use to build the technology of tomorrow."
